Mixed Doubles Rule Check
Race Structure 8 x 1km runs plus Ski Erg, Sled Push, Sled Pull, Burpee Broad Jumps, Row, Farmers Carry, Sandbag Lunges, and Wall Balls.
Mixed Doubles Weights Same as Men/Mixed Open: Sled Push 152kg, Sled Pull 103kg, Farmers Carry 2 x 24kg, Sandbag Lunges 20kg, Wall Ball 6kg.
Wall Ball Targets Mixed Doubles uses athlete-specific targets: male athlete 3.00m and female athlete 2.70m.
Team Separation Both athletes must stay within 5 seconds of each other, enter/exit stations together, and only start a workout once both are present.
